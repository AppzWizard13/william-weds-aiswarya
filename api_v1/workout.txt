# Complete Updated Fitness Programming Guide

**Important notes:**
- Warm-up: 5–10min before every session (mobility + light cardio).
- Cool-down: 5–10min after every session (stretching/breathing).
- Progressive overload: increase weight 2.5–5% or add 1–2 reps weekly if RPE ≤7.
- RPE guidance: aim RPE 6–7 (Beginner), 7–8 (Medium), 8–9 (Advanced/Master).
- Safety: Stop if sharp pain, prioritize form, adjust load for recovery and sleep.

## 1) Beginner Level

### 1.1 Weight Loss (Beginner)
**Goal:** Build habit, full-body circuits, high NEAT, moderate cardio.
**Weekly structure:** 4 full-body strength + 2 cardio sessions.

**Monday (Full-Body Circuit A)** — 45–55min
- Goblet Squat — 3×10–12 — Keep chest up, full depth — 12–15min
- Incline Push-up — 3×8–12 — Use bench to scale — 10min
- Seated Row (Machine/Band) — 3×10–12 — Squeeze scapula — 10min
- Hip Hinge (DB RDL) — 3×10 — Neutral spine — 10min
- Plank — 3×30–45s — Brace core — 8min

**Tuesday (Cardio Intervals, Low Impact)** — 30–40min
- Treadmill incline walk or cycle — 5min warm-up
- 10×1min brisk/1min easy
- 5–10min cool-down

**Wednesday (Full-Body Circuit B)** — 45–55min
- Step-ups — 3×10/leg — Knee tracks toes — 12min
- Lat Pulldown — 3×10–12 — Control down — 10min
- DB Shoulder Press — 3×8–10 — No arch — 10min
- Glute Bridge — 3×12–15 — Pause at top — 8min
- Dead Bug — 3×10/side — Low back down — 8min

**Thursday (Full-Body Circuit C)** — 45–55min
- Leg Press — 3×10–12 — Full ROM — 10min
- Chest Press (Machine/DB) — 3×8–10 — Tempo 2-1-2 — 10min
- Cable Row — 3×10–12 — Neutral wrist — 10min
- Back Extension or Bird Dog — 3×12 — Controlled — 8min
- Farmer Carry — 4×30m — Upright posture — 8min

**Friday (Full-Body Circuit D)** — 40–50min
- Wall Sit — 3×45–60s — Back flat against wall — 8min
- Modified Push-ups — 3×10–12 — Focus on form — 10min
- Assisted Pull-ups/Lat Pulldown — 3×8–10 — 10min
- Single-leg Glute Bridge — 3×10/leg — 8min
- Side Plank — 3×20–30s/side — 8min

**Saturday (Steady-State Cardio)** — 35–45min
- Choose: cycle, elliptical, row, or swim at conversational pace

**Sunday:** Rest
